IT Engagement Levels

Projects: Small-scale projects, localized implementations, isolated, or projects where standards and best practices are well-defined.

CGS IT Role: Provide relevant standards, policies, and documentation. Offer initial consultation, and be available for questions.

Example: Basic Business daily operation.

Projects: Projects requiring technical expertise not readily available in local IT.

CGS IT Role: Provide consultation, review designs, and provide recommendations on security controls, compliance, and best practices. May conduct security assessments or vulnerability testing.

Example: Implementing a new cloud platform or integrating a third-party API.

Projects: Projects with potential security implications, projects involving new technologies, or larger projects, projects with complex security requirements, or projects with high business impact. Involve external stakeholder.

CGS IT Role: Participate as a member of the project team, contribute expertise in security, compliance, and standards. May lead specific security-related tasks.

Example: Implementing a new enterprise-wide data management system or migrating to a new cloud infrastructure.

Projects: High-risk, high-impact projects, projects involving new technologies or approaches, or projects requiring strong corporate oversight.

CGS IT Role: Lead the project team, ensure adherence to standards, oversee security design and implementation, and manage security risks.

Example: Deploying a new working platform to a new business, developing a new technology, or implementing a major ERP (C-Up, F1, SAP upgrade).

Projects: Highly strategic, company-wide projects with significant security, compliance, and business impact.

CGS IT Role: Own the project, define the scope, budget, timeline, and deliverables. Lead the project team, ensure compliance, and oversee security implementation.

Example: Developing and deploying a new company-wide digital strategy or implementing a major single source data management (Data Ocean) or The complex end-to-end value chain working platform (Bluenet).
