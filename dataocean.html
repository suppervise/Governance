Our Data Journey

From Chaotic Kitchens to a Gourmet Experience

The Past: A Tale of Many Chaotic Kitchens

Siloed Ingredients

Each team had its own small, locked pantry. Data from SAP, Blunet, and Operations couldn't be mixed, leading to an incomplete picture.

Redundant Work

Every team had to wash, chop, and prep the same ingredients (data cleansing and transformation) over and over, wasting valuable time.

Slow Service

When business needed a new "dish" (report), it took weeks or months to get the ingredients and cook it, by which time the opportunity was lost.

The Present: Our "Data Ocean" Smart Kitchen

We built a centralized, state-of-the-art kitchen that serves everyone efficiently, systematically, and safely.

Fresh Ingredient Sourcing

Data Ocean automatically connects to all our sources (SAP, Blunet, etc.) to bring in fresh, diverse data ingredients 24/7.

The Ultimate Prep Station

Automated pipelines handle the hard work: cleansing, transforming, and preparing the data so it's ready to use.

A Perfectly Labeled Pantry

Our Data Catalog ensures every dataset is accessible, traceable, and reusable. No more guessing what's inside.

Health & Safety Guaranteed

Full governance and PDPA compliance are built-in, ensuring every dish is served safely and securely.

Today's Capability: Empowering Our Chefs

We've moved from cooking *for* our business units to giving them the tools to cook for themselves.

We Provide...

An endless supply of high-quality, prepped raw ingredients from the Data Ocean pantry.

You Create...

Your own amazing dishes (visuals & analytics) using professional tools like Power BI, all within a secure environment.

The Future: A Menu of Gourmet "Data Products"

Our next step is to have our domain experts—our Michelin-star chefs—prepare signature dishes that are ready to serve instantly.

S&OP Optimization Model

A complex, ready-to-use model from our S&OP experts to perfect our supply chain, served instantly.

Customer & Cost-to-Serve View

A 360° view of our customers, crafted by the Go-To-Market team, ready for strategic decision-making.

Talent Management Insights

A ready-made analytical product from HR to identify and nurture top talent across the organization.

The Business Impact

Improved Time-to-Market

By providing ready-made ingredients and gourmet dishes, we can answer business questions in days, not months, seizing opportunities faster.

Reduced Redundancy & Cost

"Cook once, serve many." Our Data Products eliminate wasted effort, allowing our brightest minds to focus on innovation, not data prep.

Our journey with Data Ocean is transforming us from a collection of kitchens into a world-class culinary enterprise, ready to serve the future.
