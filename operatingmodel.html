North Star Program: Detailed Process & IT Work Plan

To: The North Star Transformation Management Office (TMO) and Steering Committee

From: Boondee, Lead Consultant, Process & IT Initiative (6.2)

Date: September 15, 2025

Subject: Detailed Two-Period Work Plan for Process & IT Transformation

This document outlines the detailed work plan for the Process & IT initiative, structured into two distinct periods as requested. This approach facilitates parallel progress between business-led process design and foundational IT optimization, ensuring we deliver immediate value through cost savings while preparing for large-scale implementation in the new year.

Period 1: Detailed Design & Foundational Optimization (Mid-September - End of Year 2025)

Primary Objectives: To finalize the "To-Be" detailed design in collaboration with the business, achieve tangible cost savings through IT rationalization, and prepare for execution by engaging and assessing an implementation partner. We will deliver immediate business support through targeted enhancements for order fulfillment and data integration.
Timeline Workstream Key Activities Key Deliverables Lead / Involved Teams
Weeks 1-6
(Mid-Sep to Oct)		 Business-Led Design Support Participate in workshops for O2C, Supply Chain, and P2P process mapping. Provide real-time advice on technology feasibility and constraints. IT Feasibility Assessments for "As-Is" process pain points. Business Architecture, Application Architecture
IT Optimization & Strategy Initiate comprehensive inventory of all applications. Conduct interviews and use discovery tools to build the application portfolio. Begin redundancy and overlap analysis. Draft Application Portfolio Register. Application Architecture, Technology Architecture
Cross-Functional Enablement Define data domains and key entities required for E2E processes (e.g., Customer, Product, Order). Draft initial IT governance principles and security standards. Conceptual Data Model; Draft Governance Charter. Data Architecture, Governance, Security
Weeks 7-12
(Nov to Mid-Dec)		 Business-Led Design Support Co-design "To-Be" processes with business teams. Model future-state scenarios, define business rules, and map new authority matrix requirements to system capabilities. Identify enhancement needs for existing apps. Detailed Design Document (IT sections); Change Requests for existing apps (Order Fulfillment). Business Architecture, Application Architecture, Business Units
IT Optimization & Strategy Finalize application portfolio analysis. Classify applications (Retain, Replace, Retire). Identify and quantify cost-saving opportunities (e.g., license optimization, server decommissioning). Shortlist strategic applications to drive the new business model. Application Rationalization Roadmap; Quantified Cost-Saving Report. Application Architecture, Finance, Governance
Cross-Functional Enablement Design target data integration flows for management reporting. Propose technology architecture (Cloud vs. On-premise strategy) to support new business processes. Refine security framework. Data Integration Blueprint (Phase 1); Technology Architecture Principles. Data Architecture, Technology Architecture (Cloud, Infra, AI)
Implementation Partner Engagement Develop RFI/RFP for a software developer / systems integrator. Conduct initial vendor briefings based on the emerging "To-Be" design. Draft RFI/RFP Document; Longlist of potential partners. TMO, IT Leadership
Weeks 13-14
(End of Dec)		 Finalization & Planning Consolidate all detailed designs. Finalize the implementation partner assessment criteria. Develop the detailed project and resource plan for Period 2. Present final Period 1 outcomes to Steering Committee. Consolidated "To-Be" Architecture Design; Partner Assessment Scorecard; Period 2 Execution Plan. All IT Teams, TMO

Period 2: Implementation & Transition to New Operating Model (Q1 2026 Onwards)

Primary Objectives: To operate under the new organizational structure, execute the implementation roadmap defined in Period 1, deploy new applications and workarounds, and continuously monitor progress against the transformation goals.
Timeline Workstream Key Activities Key Deliverables / Milestones Lead / Involved Teams
Q1 2026 Organizational Transition Formally launch the new Process & IT organizational structure. Transition teams and individuals into their new roles. Cascade communication and conduct role-specific training. New Operating Model is Live. TMO, HR, IT Leadership
MVP Implementation Onboard selected implementation partner. Initiate agile sprints for developing/configuring high-priority applications (e.g., new CRM module, data platform). Deploy enhancements and workarounds for existing systems. First set of features for new applications deployed; Interim process workarounds are operational. Implementation Partner, BA/AA, Data/Tech Arch
Governance & Monitoring Convene the first Architecture Review Board (ARB) meeting. Begin monitoring the performance of new/enhanced systems. Track realization of cost savings identified in Period 1. ARB Established; First Monthly Performance & Value Realization Report. Governance, TMO, Finance
Q2 2026 Onwards Full-Scale Revamp & Rollout Continue implementation sprints, expanding functionality across business units. Begin the phased decommissioning of legacy applications as per the rationalization roadmap. Major application releases; First legacy system successfully retired. Implementation Partner, BA/AA Teams
Continuous Improvement Gather user feedback on new systems and processes. Manage the backlog of change requests and enhancements. Continuously optimize cloud resource usage (FinOps). User Feedback & Satisfaction Scorecard; Optimized Cloud Spend Report. All IT Teams, Business Process Owners

This plan provides a clear, actionable path forward. It balances the immediate needs of the business with the long-term strategic imperative to build a lean, modern, and agile Process & IT function. I am confident that by following this structured approach, we will successfully deliver the foundational capabilities required for the North Star program.

Boondee
