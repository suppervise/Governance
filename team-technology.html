Technology Architecture Team

Driving innovation and efficiency through a resilient, scalable, and future-proof technology foundation (Infrastructure, Cloud, AI & Emerging Tech).

Team Overview & Rationale

Mission: To design, implement, secure, and continuously evolve a resilient, scalable, and cost-effective technology foundation (Infrastructure, Cloud, AI) that enables CGS's digital ambitions, drives innovation, and identifies/integrates impactful emerging technologies.

Why This Team is Essential: Without this consolidated team, CGS would face fragmented technology strategies, inefficient resource utilization, slower adoption of new technologies, missed innovation opportunities, and potentially higher operational risks and costs. This team ensures a cohesive and future-proof technology landscape.

Core Team Structure & Key Roles:
  • Chief Technology Architect
  • Lead Infrastructure Architect
  • Lead Cloud Architect
  • Lead AI Architect/Engineer
  • Emerging Technology Lead/Analyst
  • Security Architects (Embedded/Collaborating)
Required Skills:

Expertise in Infrastructure, Cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP, IaC, FinOps), AI (ML, MLOps, AI Platforms), EA principles, technology integration, security, project management, technology foresight.

36-Month Action Plan & Roadmap

Overall Goal / End-State Vision: CGS possesses a highly optimized, secure, and agile technology ecosystem where Infrastructure, Cloud, and AI capabilities are seamlessly integrated, efficiently managed, and strategically leveraged to accelerate business innovation. A proactive technology foresight function continuously identifies and integrates next-generation technologies.

Infrastructure (Q1-Q2):

Complete Infra assessment, stabilize critical systems. Define tiered infra standards.

Cloud (Q1-Q2):

Finalize Cloud Adoption Framework & Governance. Implement foundational FinOps.

AI (Q1-Q2):

Establish AI Governance Framework. Identify initial high-impact AI use cases.

Cross-Cutting (Q3-Q4):

Develop an integrated Technology Architecture Blueprint V1.0. Establish a formal Emerging Technology Scouting & Evaluation process. Begin cross-skilling initiatives.

Key Milestones:

First AI pilot launched. Foundational FinOps implemented.

Key Initiatives: Infra modernization, Cloud migration & advanced FinOps, AI pilot projects & MLOps, Pilot emerging technologies, Optimize resource utilization.

Key Initiatives: Optimized hybrid/multi-cloud, Scale successful AI solutions, Integrate successful emerging tech pilots, Continuous innovation pipeline, Develop advanced capabilities (Agentic AI, MCP).

Key Milestones: Measurable cost savings from cloud optimization (M24). Scaled AI solution deployed (M24). Successful integration of piloted emerging tech (M30). CGS recognized for leveraging advanced tech (M36).

Measurable Outcomes & KPIs:
  • Reduction in overall IT infrastructure TCO by X%.
  • Improvement in system uptime and resilience (X% reduction in critical incidents).
  • Successful deployment of Y AI solutions delivering measurable business value.
  • Adoption rate of new cloud services by Z%.
  • Number of emerging technologies evaluated and piloted annually.
  • Time to provision new technology resources reduced by X%.

Business Impact

  • Accelerated Innovation: Faster adoption of new and emerging technologies.
  • Enhanced Agility & Scalability: Modern infrastructure and cloud enable rapid scaling.
  • Cost Optimization: Efficient management of resources reduces operational costs.
  • Improved Operational Resilience: Robust infrastructure and DR/BCP.
  • Data-Powered Insights & Automation: AI capabilities unlock insights and automate tasks.
  • Future-Proofing: Proactive technology scouting ensures CGS remains competitive.
