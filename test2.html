Strategy 1: Establish a Strong Foundational Architecture

Building a secure, resilient, and efficient platform for the entire Group.

The "Why" Behind Our Foundation

Need: To manage rising complexity, cybersecurity threats, and regulatory demands (like PDPA) in a standardized way.
Benefit: Reduced operational risk, lower costs through standardization, improved security posture, and faster delivery of future projects.
Purpose: To create a reliable "digital engine" that enables the entire Group to operate safely and efficiently.

H1 Achievements (What We've Done)

  • Application: Established Guidelines & Standards; Achieved Application Portfolio Visibility.
  • Data: Rolled out PDPA Program: กำหนด standard เอกสารที่เกี่ยวข้อง, กำหนด DPO แต่ละบริษัท, Training กฎหมาย PDPA, Workshop PDP Health Check, PDP Drill Test (Data Leak & Ransomware).
  • Technology:
    - Infra: Segmented standards by business size; Test health check at ToongSong Plant.
    - Cloud: Set up foundational cloud standards.
    - AI: Established a "Do, Don't" policy to guide responsible AI adoption.
  • Governance: Group-wide Awareness & Communication campaign; Software piracy cleansing initiative.

H2 Roadmap (What's Next)

  • Application: Conduct comprehensive VA Scan and remediate all critical gaps.
  • Data: Enhance Data Ocean to comply with Data Governance (e.g., Data Masking, Data Classification).
  • Technology:
    - Infra: WAN-to-WAN improvement project for Cement plants.
    - Cloud: Gain full visibility and implement multi-cloud optimization strategies.
    - AI: Conduct hands-on workshops to increase AI adoption and capability.
  • Governance: Implement full PC Lifecycle Management and a centralized Software Inventory tool.

Strategy 2: Drive Business Value with Digital Initiatives

Leveraging our digital capabilities to achieve strategic objectives and create a competitive edge.

The "Why" Behind Our Initiatives

Need: To respond to market demands, improve operational efficiency, and unlock new revenue streams for our diverse business units.
Benefit: Increased revenue, improved margins, better customer experience, data-driven decision-making, and sustainable growth.
Purpose: To be a proactive strategic partner, co-creating digital solutions that help our businesses win in their respective markets.

H1 Achievements (What We've Delivered)

  • Cement: Provided foundational Data Set for their digital transformation.
  • Concrete: Cloud Migration (Presale, Product, e-Business, e-Maintenance); ML for Strength Prediction.
  • Green Circularity: Delivered Waste Management Data platform.
  • Regional Business: Launched e-Purchasing & K-Concrete platform.
  • CGS Group: Deployed Justperform for domestic, ESG Platform, and AI Projects (Kitcarbon, Risk Insight, Legal Eye).

H2 Roadmap (What's Coming)

  • Cement: Migrate legacy CDAS database, MIS, and QIS systems.
  • Concrete: Cloud Migration for Sale & Dispatch modules; New Transportation Cost Structure.
  • Green Circularity: Launch RDF Trading & Supply platform in Indonesia.
  • Green Industrial: Stream Providing project for Micheline Group.
  • Regional Business: Continue to enhance IT Management; Begin SAP Vendor Transition.
  • CGS Group: Extend Justperform scope; Customer 360 initiative; AI Standard Platform; Enhancement from North Star project.
